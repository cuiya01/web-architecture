About This Project

UCL has 18,000 international students from outside the UK this year. Besides of studying, most international students also want to explore London's famous attractions. However, finding destinations often requires switching between different applications and web pages, which international students are unfamiliar with them. So, we intended to develop a website that could combine the data and resources we all need to take our trips from idea to execution, helping simplify the trip planning process for students.

In this project, we mainly used the API of google map, google trends, Instagram, and some public data sets to collect the list of attractions, search popularity, number of crimes in a specific area, transportation methods, photos and pictures of tourist spots, and other resources that users are most concerned about. We hope to help users find the destinations they want to visit and plan their trips reasonably.
