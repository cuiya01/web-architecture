WHAT YOU CONCERN London Criminal Frequency



The data of crime heat map comes from London datastore website . Borough Level Crime in London from 01/11/2019 to 31/10/2021 is recorded in the database. The type and location of the crime are listed. Taking the location as the unit, the record of the number of crimes in the same borough in recent two years is shown in the figure. The heat map was chosen to visualize the data, since the bright and changeable colours can intuitively show the difference in the number of crimes geographically.
In the initial map, red represents the large number of crimes, and cold blue represents the small number of crimes. A rolling wheel is set here! How interesting! By rolling the wheel, the colour of the centre, middle, and end of the heat map can be changed. At the same time, the heat map radius and transparency can also be changed in the map, this is a very interesting feature, come and experience it!
